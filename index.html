SimCav

SimCav is a program for designing laser cavities and analysing the beam geometry inside them

Powerful and portable

With its cavity calculator, you no longer will spend hours finding the correct configuration. SimCav can do it for you in seconds. No installation is required, just download and run.

Simple and intuitive

Modern and straightforward graphical interface working with one mouse click. Export your cavity designs as jpg, png, etc. for convenient saving/printing.

Free software

SimCav respects your freedom. You can run, copy, distribute, study, change and improve the software. Besides being free as in "free speech", SimCav is also free as in "free beer"!

Windows policy:

While a SimCav version for Microsoft Windows is available, I would prefer you not to use it. Microsoft is quite hostile to free software and I would prefer you to run SimCav on a platform that is not controlled by a free-software-hostile corporation.

Apple policy:

SimCav can be run on MacOS, but I would prefer you not to do that. Apple is even more hostile than Microsoft to free software, technically and legally trapping cumsumers and developpers in a (golden?) cage. If you are unfortunate enough to already own Apple products, please consider switching to an open platform like GNU/Linux or FreeBSD that doesn't impose 1984-like restrictions on your freedom.
