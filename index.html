SimCav

SimCav is a program for designing laser cavities and for analysing the beam geometry inside such optical resonator.

Powerful

With its cavity calculator, no longer will you spend hours finding the correct configuration.

Simple and intuitive

Straightforward graphical interface working on mouse clicking.
